* Tank Garchomp build Pokemon generation 6 from Smogon, [http://www.smogon.com/dex/xy/pokemon/garchomp/](http://www.smogon.com/dex/xy/pokemon/garchomp/).
* Moves.
    * Dragon Tail.

        * Speed 16.
